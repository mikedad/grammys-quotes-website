Honoring Grammy's Legacy

In 2018, Carole Lemp created a wonderful memoir called "Grammy's Little Book of Quotes and Special Memories." The memoir captures not only her life's journey but also her favorite quotes that brought her wisdom and joy.

Reading her book recently, I appreciate it on a new level and am so grateful that she took the time and care to create it. Throughout the book, she sprinkled many of her favorite quotes. I thought that, rather than trying to absorb them all at once, it might be a lovely way to honor her, and allow for one to appreciate each quote individually, if they were viewed one at a time.

This was the inspiration for Grammy's Quotes: a way to honor her memory and allow each quote to be appreciated in its own moment, delivered to you daily as a gentle reminder of the wisdom she treasured.

Grammy was a nature lover who found joy in trees, flowers, bees, butterflies, and all of life's interconnected beauty.
